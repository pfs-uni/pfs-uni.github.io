!BuyShite
Overview

!BuyShite is a phone app and browser extension to help you save money! Australians love to gamble, so we are leveraging that knowledge to help incentivise members to save money and not spend it. We are applying the approach of a 'No Lose Lottery' with a twist to help build the savings of low income Australians.

Everytime you are about to buy something, our app !BuyShite jumps in and asks you if you really need to buy it. We then offer you the alternative to put the money or some additional amount into a savings account.

Here's the great part, every time you deposit money into the savings account, you increase your chances to win a monthly lottery for people who deposit savings!

Motivation

Many Australians don't save enough money to handle even small emergencies. I know what that feels like because I've been there too. There are also a proliferation of 'fintech' services encouraging everyone to buy now and pay later.

It doesn't have to be this way though. 'No Lose Lotteries' have been proven to be succesful ways to increase the savings of all kinds of people but especially those on low incomes.

Lotteries, as they exist today, only extract money out of people. However, many people with low incomes feel this is the only way they will every have the opportunity to make a large enough sum of money. We think we can help change that with our app !BuyShite

Description

The question you are probably asking right now is, "What is a No Lose Lottery? This Freakonomics podcast will help explain --> Freakonomics - No Lose Lottery

In a nutshell, its a way to incentivise savings by using peoples love of gambling in a positive way.

There are two parts to the application

  1. Phone Application
  2. Web Browser Add-In

Features the application will require are:

  • Detect when a shopping cart order is about to be placed.
  • Notify the user and ask if they really want to purchase the item.
    • If yes, give them the opportunity to deposit the rounding difference
    • If no, ask if they would like to deposit the full amount of the purchase into the saving account instead.
  • Track all spending below $20.00
  • Set savings goals
  • Show details about people who have won each month
  • Show the amount possible to win
  • Link to financial institution
  • Show how much money has been saved
    • In total
    • per month
    • saved because of cancelled spending
    • saved when rounding up purchases

Some of the obstacles will be determining what financial rules and laws will be applicable. Lotteries are State run and a major source of revenue, this could present a problem if they feel this reduces their potential income. Privacy is also a major concern as we are dealing with sensitive financial data.

Tools & Technologies
This application requires only software to be developed. Initially, the application will be written in HTML5, CSS and JavaScript for the user interface and browser extension. Due to the security concerns, an application will probably need to be developed for each type of phone operatings system the application will run on.
Skills Required
Most of the phone application and web coding skills are reasonably common to find today. Integration with other services is likely to be beneficial, so a solid understanding of how financial applications work will be a high priority. In order to reach the most number of people, it will be necessary to also be able to reach out to groups and organisations whose social goals may be aligned with ours.
Outcome

Successful implementation of this project could have far reaching consequences. The problem will never likely be 'solved' but will be an ongoing challenge.

Uptake of the application will help provide a way to boost Australians savings mentality and reduce the day to day stress many of our fellow citizens feel. Increasing happiness and a sense of security and control over ones life are not unreasonable or insignificant expected outcomes.
