Job Description and what makes this job interesting

The job I have selected as a 'dream' job is as a consultant with open source giant Redhat, now part of IBM. As a consultant you are required to maintain good contact and relationships with a variety of clients. A high level of technical skill is also required in order that you can provide customers with answers and give them the confidence that you can support their varied needs.

What I find appealing about a role like this is that you need to be technically skilled but you also have contact with a wide variety of people. It's a real benefit about consulting work that you are able to assist your customers to reach their goals. The varied nature of the work, troubleshooting, researching and thinking about how to best help a client before they even know it, is exciting and rewarding.

Job Requirements
  • A solid understanding of Redhat's business offerings.
  • Good interpersonal skills.
  • An ability to quickly understand a problem and architect solutions.
  • Excellent written and verbal skills.
  • Identify potential sales opportunities.
  • Technical skills in scripting, network authentication and security, virtualisation and Linux package management
Current Skills
Currently, I have good written and verbal skills. I am experienced in developing solid, mutually beneficial, long term customer relationships. Most technical areas required improvement and more direct commercial experience. I have reasonably good scripting skills and base line linux administration skills.
